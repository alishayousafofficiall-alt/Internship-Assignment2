About Weather App

Our Mission

The Weather App provides accurate and real-time weather updates for cities worldwide. Our goal is to make weather information simple, quick, and visually appealing.

Features

  • Real-time temperature, humidity, and weather conditions.
  • Dynamic weather icons and background images.
  • User-friendly interface for all devices.
  • Backgrounds powered by Unsplash API.

Why Choose Us?

Combining simplicity with aesthetics, our app ensures that weather info is easy to read and visually pleasing for work, travel, or leisure.
